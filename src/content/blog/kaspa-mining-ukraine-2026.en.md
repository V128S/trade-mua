Kaspa (KAS) has been one of the most talked-about coins in the mining community over the past two years. ## Why Is Kaspa Attracting Attention?

- **Transaction confirmation speed** — Kaspa's blockchain uses GHOSTDAG technology and confirms transactions in seconds.
- **Unique algorithm** — KHeavyHash doesn't compete with Bitcoin or Litecoin, so ASIC manufacturers have focused on purpose-built solutions.
- **High liquidity** — KAS ranks in the top 50 cryptocurrencies by market cap and trades on all major exchanges.

## Kaspa Mining Risks

1. **KAS volatility** — the coin price can drop significantly, erasing profit.
2. **Difficulty growth** — as new ASICs come online, network difficulty rises and each machine earns less.
3. **Narrow market** — KHeavyHash hardware is harder to resell than SHA-256 machines.
4. **Equipment price volatility** — KS7 prices correlate with KAS price.
