The Antminer S21 and S23 are Bitmain's two current Bitcoin mining series. They may look similar at first glance, but there are meaningful differences in efficiency, price, and use cases. Here's a breakdown.

## Key Specifications

| Model | Hashrate | Power | Efficiency | Approx. Price |
|-------|----------|-------|------------|---------------|
| Antminer S21 | 200 TH/s | 3500 W | 17.5 J/TH | from $2,500 |
| Antminer S21 Pro | 234 TH/s | 3510 W | 15 J/TH | from $3,200 |
| Antminer S21 Hydro | 335 TH/s | 5360 W | 16 J/TH | from $8,000 |
| Antminer S23 | 270 TH/s | 3486 W | 12.9 J/TH | from $5,500 |
| Antminer S23 Hydro | 380 TH/s | 5520 W | 14.5 J/TH | from $10,000 |

## How the Series Differ

**S21** — a proven series with a solid price-to-efficiency balance. The S21 Pro delivers a noticeable efficiency jump (+14% vs. base S21) with virtually no difference in power draw. Suitable for most private and small-scale industrial setups.

**S23** — next-generation hardware. At 12.9 J/TH, it's the best air-cooled efficiency in 2026. At tariffs above $0.07/kWh, the daily net profit difference between S21 Pro and S23 becomes meaningful.

**Hydro versions** (S21 Hydro, S23 Hydro) — for industrial mining hotels with liquid cooling infrastructure. Much higher hashrate, but require specialized cooling systems.

## What to Choose at Different Electricity Rates

**Under $0.06/kWh** — S21 or S21 Pro will pay back faster thanks to the lower purchase price. The efficiency gap between generations is not critical at this rate.

**$0.07–0.10/kWh** — S23 justifies the premium. Better efficiency directly translates to lower daily electricity costs.

**Above $0.10/kWh** — older models become unprofitable. You need S23 or an industrial electricity rate.

## Which Cooling to Choose

- **Air cooling** (standard S21/S23) — works in most environments, easier to maintain.
- **Hydro** — only for mining hotels with liquid cooling. Delivers 30–40% more hashrate in the same footprint, but requires dedicated cooling infrastructure.

## Bottom Line

- **Limited budget (under $3,000)** → Antminer S21
- **Best value (under $4,000)** → Antminer S21 Pro
- **Maximum efficiency, budget $5,000+** → Antminer S23
- **Industrial mining hotel** → S21 Hydro or S23 Hydro
